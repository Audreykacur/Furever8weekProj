A Guide to Furever


Hello and welcome to Furever. We look forward to working with you in the process of finding your forever friend. Our user side is for individuals looking to adopt a pet. Shortly we will have the shelter side up and running, with features to come. If you would like to add your pet to Furever then please message our animal support and they will love to help you with that process.

Once you click on our website URL: There will be a button located in the center of the page displaying “Join Furever”. Clicking this button will take you to the Join page which will prompt you to enter your information. This information that will help match you to the puurfect pet of your dreams. After entering all required information, press the button on the bottom left that says “submit” at the bottom of the Join page.

Once you click on our website URL: a button on the top right says “Log in”. This is the button you will click if you have previously created an account with Furever and wish to log back in. Do I have to create an account to view the current animals? Every individual who wishes to view the furry friends on Furever, must create a profile to view all their fluffy, scaly, and feathery faces.

Furever will display animals that our current algorithm matches (ex. animals requirements with your information). If you like the furbaby that displays on your screen we ask that you swipe right. There is no way to display previously displayed animals that were not swiped right on. You can swipe right on whichever animals you find to be favorable that match your requirements. The owner of that current pet will then be able to see your name, phone number and they will connect you if a match is made.

Currently there is no limit. However, please only swipe right on fur babies that you are serious about adopting. If we are notified that you are spamming adoption agencies and not following through, your account will be blocked and you will be notified.

Email our team at FureverAdoptionSupport@gmail.com we ask for your agencies name, phone number, email, and an idea of how many pets you would like to add to our site. We will then verify that you are a credible organization and connect with you further to create the best user experience for you. We offer training/documentation to help you use our application that is offered the first weekend of every month and if scheduled whenever is best for you.
