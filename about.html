What is Furever?

An increasing number of people stayed home and began working from home during 2020 and 2021.
The ASPA estimated based on a survey that during this time approximately 23 million American homes
adopted an animal or pet. As the prevalence of working from home increases, people seek companionship
in the form of a family pet to have near them. Traditionally adoption of pets is completed in two ways,
one: visiting shelters online or in person, and two: adopting from people in local areas through personal
contacts of social media. Additionally, There is no centralized or mainstream method to easily find family
pets and see if those pets are a match for adoption like there are for other services like dating or finding
homes. Modern day society is all about creating the most of your time and spending the rest of your time
with your loved ones. We have taken the needs and wants of millions wishing to adopt a pet and the
requirement of the modern day idea of making life easier, to create Furever.

Furever is a service that allows individuals seeking pets
for adoption to view and match with pets being listed
by adoption agencies or people seeking to rehome their pets.

How does it work

Adopters: Individuals seeking to adopt a family pet.
Adopters will be able to independently sign up for an account and
enter their personal information and basic requirements for the
animals they wish to seek. These requirements will later be used in a Furever
filtering system to connect them with a pet that fills those needs.
The adopter will be granted access to search for their furever pet by viewing
pets posted for adoption on a card format, on which they will swipe right to
indicate they like the shown pet option, or left to indicate they do not want the
shown pet option.
If you have any addition questions head over to the FAQ page or contact customer support

Sellers: Any individual wishing to rehome an animal they are
currently in possession of. Our Furever application is set
up where any individual wishing to add a pet for adoption can
contact us to verify their credibility. We verify adoption agencies,
humane societies, and all animal shelters, along with individuals who are
not associated with an agency wishing to rehome their current family
pet. All those verified have access to add animals to our site under their
username. Furever does not wish to give for-profit pet breeders
a place where they can easily sell their dogs for money.
